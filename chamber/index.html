City needs to improve revenue situation

In its special session on Monday, the city council partially approved the measures proposed by the administration to improve the city's income situation. The committee voted for an increase in the resident parking fees and an increase in the assessment rates for property and trade tax. According to the resolution, the introduction of a packaging tax is to be examined by the administration. The decision to introduce an accommodation tax was postponed. The introduction of a fee for winter service on road surfaces was rejected. more info...
