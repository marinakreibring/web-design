Hello! My name is Marina Kreibring.

I speak Russian and Belarusian (native), English (C1-C2), and German (B1 and learning further).

I worked at school as an English teacher for 10 years. I taught English to students from grades 1 through 8, designing and delivering engaging lessons that fostered language development and critical thinking. My work experience allowed me to develop leadership and organizational skills in addition to my knowledge of languages.

During my years of study and work, I have developed strong skills in troubleshooting, teamwork and collaboration, time management, interpersonal communication, problem-solving, and adaptability. I am an open, reliable, responsible, and team-oriented professional who thrives in dynamic environments.
